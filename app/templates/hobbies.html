My hobbies span a few areas, but overall, I am a quite indoor person. I like to read, watch movies and anime, listen to music (more on that in the music section), solve soduku puzzles and watch shows online (of course). I am also a foodie. I always have a genuine interest and curiosity for food that I have not tried before.
My music taste changes quite a bit over years as well. Generally, I listen to pop music in both Chinese and English, and whatever kind of music I find online. In addition, I also really like kpop and track music from movies, shows and anime.
